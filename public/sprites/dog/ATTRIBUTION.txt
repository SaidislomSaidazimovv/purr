Same pack as public/sprites/cat/ — this is the Dog character, previously
unused. Used from the dog character (renamed to 1.png, 2.png, ... in
original frame order):
- Idle -> idle/
- Walk -> walk/
- Fall -> fall/
- Jump -> happy/ (excited pose, shown when mood is happy and idle)
- Hurt -> grumpy/ (upset pose, shown when mood is grumpy and idle)

The Dead/Run/Slide animations were not used (matches the cat skin's
unused set).
